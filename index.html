INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Take or obtain an image of something meaningful to you.

2. Upload your image to this website. A new image will be automatically generated. (Nothing will be saved or collected, this program runs locally).

3. Save and do whatever you'd like with the image.

Some suggestions include:
- Print it out and use it like a talisman.
- Make up a ritual and burn it.
- Delete it.
- Use it in a collage.
- Refragment the refragmented image.

Only you can see this image. It's yours now.

Want to share?
I've been gradually collecting refragmented images that have been shared with me in preparation for a mini-gallery on this website. If you'd like to be featured in it, feel free to share with me:

1. your refragmented image

2. a brief story behind why you chose what you did

3. your first name (or Anonymous if you'd prefer).

Materials can be submitted via email (achen05@mica.edu) with "Refragmenter" as the subject line.
